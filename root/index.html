What Is Ultimate?


Combining the non-stop movement and athletic endurance of soccer with the aerial passing skills of football, played by two seven-player squads, with a high-tech plastic disc on a field similar to football. The objective is to score by catching a pass in the opponent's end zone.

Ultimate is played in over 42 countries by hundreds of thousands of men and women, girls and boys.


About Us:


We are Wilmington Youth Ultimate Training (WYUT). Our mission is to showcase the exciting sport of ultimate frisbee to the youth of Wilmington. We want all middle school and high school level athletes to have a fun experience while learning the basics of this amazing sport. North Carolina Ultimate has one of the best youth scenes in the world, and it's about time Wilmington jumps into the mix.
